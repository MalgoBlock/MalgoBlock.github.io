Instructions

The purpose of the game is to disarm all four bombs before the 100 seconds time runs out.
Press 'New game' button to start the timer and to unlock the first bomb. To open the bomb press , the next bomb will get unlocked when you disarm the first one.
To disarm the bomb you have to follow the clues. To access each clue you need to press at the bottom edge of the bomb which will display the clue.
After you successfully cut the first wire, the clue arrow will turn green and if the bomb is not yet disarmed the next arrow will light up yellow.
Each clue consist of the position of the wire (e.g. 'Second') and a number. The number is the sum or the multiplication of the numbers on the dice connected by the wire. The multiplication is marked by a *. The position describes which wire counting from the left with the corresponding result is the one to cut.
To cut the wire simply click on it.
You win the game if you disarm all four bombs within the time limit.
If you cut incorrect wire or run our of time the bombs will explode and you lose the game.
