Pick your colors:

The aim of this game is to guess a sequence of six colors.
You have 12 attempts to guess the sequence, starting at the row 1 at the bottom of the screen.
After each attempt clues are displayed to aid the guessing: black for a right color in a right position and white for a right color in incorrect position.
If you decide to stop the game you can reveal the solution at the top of the board.

This game is based on a 1970s Mastermind game designed by Mordecai Meirowitz.
For more details see Wikipedia page about Mastermind.
